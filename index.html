ORDER

Order your printing part here. Please note that we cannot print parts bigger than 210x210x250. If you haven't designed your part yet and want to know how, you can learn it here.

Pricing: We charge a general fee of 30 birr per print. Additionally it costs 35 birr per hour it takes to finish your print.
Example: If your print takes two hours, the price is: 30 birr + 70 birr (2 x 35 birr) = 100 birr

Attention: The form opens your email application with the data you entered. To complete the order you have to attach your 3d-printing file and send the email!
